<h2 id="study" class="spacer-small">Study overview</h2> 
<p>This is a high-level overview of the steps involved in the research. </p><p>For full information, please review the Participant Information section below.</p>
</div>

<div class="row justify-content-left mt-lg-3 spacer-small"> 
    <div class="col-lg-4"> 
            <img src="/images/feather/icons/edit_note.svg" alt="" width="80"/>
        <h2 class="h3 spacer-header">1. Make content </h3>
        <p>Complete our short interest form, to arrange an initial chat and so we understand the learning content you need.</p><p>Kita will build learning content based on your specifications.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="col-lg-4"> 
            <img src="/images/feather/icons/chrome_reader_mode.svg" alt="" width="80"/>
        <h2 class="h3 spacer-header">2. Review docs </h3>
        <p>We are actively adding documentation to help support your school's policies and requirements.</p> 
        <br/>
        <p>Check our our <a href="#bank">document bank</a> below and let us know if you need anything else.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="col-lg-4"> 
            <img src="/images/feather/icons/assured_workload.svg" alt="" width="80"/>
        <h2 class="h3 spacer-header">3. Give consent </h3>
        <p>We will arrange the consent process with you based on the needs of your school.</p> <br/>
        <p>Our processes are currently being reviewed to ensure that meet legal and DfE requirements.</p>
    </div> 
</div>

<div class="row justify-content-left mt-lg-3">
    <div class="col-lg-4">
        <h2 class="h3">4. Take part</h3>
        <ul>
                <li>Complete a pre-survey and a post-survey. </li> 
                <li>The pilot is planned to run in June 2026. </li>
                <li>Use the Kita platform for one assigned activity (est. 50 minutes). </li> 
                <li>Use Kita for formative assessment and returning feedback to students. </li> 
        </ul> 
    </div>
    <div class="col-lg-4 spacer-section">
        <h2 class="h3">5. Outcomes</h3>
        <p>The main deliverables from the research are listed below, these will be shared with participating schools:
        </p> 
        <ul>
        <li>Evaluation report</li> 
        <li>Summaries for schools and teachers</li>
        </ul>
</div>
